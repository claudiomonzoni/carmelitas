About Carmelitas

Carmelitas Café is a regional Mexican food restaurant, located in Ixtapa Zihuatanejo, Guerrero in Mexico. In the last 10 years, Carmelitas Café has been characterized by its traditional and homemade flavors and friendly service. Its dishes come from fresh and regional produce. Carmelitas Café has been characterized by the smile and the hug that Carmelita always welcomes you.

Carmelita is the owner of this restaurant, but she also is the star chef and the soul of every dish at your table.

Carmelita had transformed her restaurant into a home, an experience, a memorable story for all those who want to be surprised. There’s no doubt that Carmelitas Café is the gastronomic representation of the Guerrero coast culture.

Carmelitas Café is proud to welcome you with the regional typical dishes and an endless quantity of ingredients and local produce that are cooked with dedication and love. With drops of love. This love can be inhaled, eaten, hugged and we can feel it in every corner of this home, your home, this café, this gastronomic paradise of Carmelita and her family.
